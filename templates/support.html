How to use

Steps to upload an image/video:

1. Go to upload page.
2. Click on browse and upload the desired image/video.
3. Click on Detect my mood button.
4. If you wish to save, click on Upload without a song button.
5. If you wish to add a song, click on Choose a song button.
6. Pick a song from the list and click on Upload.
7. You can find your item in the gallery.

Note - Allowed file extensions: png, jpg, jpeg, mp4
